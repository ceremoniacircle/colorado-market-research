Dynamic Operating Expense Model

Ceremonia Spaces

1. Project Overview

Business Model: A "turnkey" facility rental model providing compliant, premium spaces to independent licensed facilitators. The facility scales in three phases, from 4 rooms to 12 rooms.

OpEx Philosophy: The model is dynamic, separating costs into three categories (Fixed, Tiered, and Variable) to accurately reflect how expenses scale with business activity. The Shared Services Fee, in particular, adjusts downward as the facility matures and requires less intensive corporate oversight.

C. Variable Costs

Costs incurred only when a room is used, tying expenses directly to revenue-generating activity.

Expense Line Item Cost Driver Rationale & Components
