Built by a Facilitator, For Facilitators.

Ceremonia Spaces was born from direct experience. Its founder, Colorado's first licensed Legacy Healer, saw a community of gifted practitioners hindered by the logistical realities of business—the search for compliant space, the administrative burdens, and the professional isolation. It was a distraction from the real work.

This is not just a real estate venture; it's a platform built to solve our collective problem, designed to uphold the integrity of the work and empower the practitioners who do it.

Our flagship Golden facility: 12 individual & 2 group rooms, just 20 minutes from downtown Denver with stunning mountain views.

The Platform for Your Professional Practice

We've addressed the critical infrastructure gaps so you can practice with confidence and focus.

The Sanctuary: Space to Practice

End the search for compliant space. Our Golden facility provides on-demand access to 12 individual rooms and is the **only licensed healing center in the Denver metro with a rentable group space**. Our nature-integrated setting provides a powerful therapeutic container for your clients.

The Operations: Turnkey Support

Your expertise is facilitation, not administration. Our platform provides a fully integrated, turnkey solution—from HIPAA-compliant EHR and billing to insurance support and regulatory peace of mind. You don't build a business; you plug into one.

The Ecosystem: Professional Community

Step out of isolation and into a vetted community of your peers. Benefit from our client referral pipeline, co-facilitation opportunities, and a network dedicated to elevating the standard of care in Colorado.

A Space for Collective Healing

Beyond individual sessions, our purpose-built group ceremony hall provides a unique and essential space for collective work. It is the only rentable, licensed group space of its kind in the region, designed to comfortably accommodate up to 16 participants.

This versatile sanctuary is ideal for facilitator trainings, group retreats, workshops, and community events, empowering you to scale your impact and diversify your offerings in a professional, inspiring environment.
