Project Overview

Mission & Model

To provide a turnkey, compliant, and premium "WeWork-style" facility for licensed facilitators. Ceremonia Spaces (the "OpCo") manages the facility and amenities.

Property & Lease

The OpCo leases the 7,062 sq ft property at 538 Commons Drive, Golden, CO, from a separate SPV via a Triple Net (NNN) lease, paying all property-related expenses.

Phased Growth Plan

The facility launches with 4 individual and 1 group room, scaling dynamically to a full capacity of 12 individual and 2 group rooms based on demand.

Core Assumptions

Revenue

  • Guaranteed: ~$18,794/month baseline.
  • Growth Scenarios: Low (+4/mo), Base (+8/mo), High (+16/mo).
  • Pricing: Standard ($497), Premium ($650), Group ($2,000).
  • Seasonality: 65% slump factor on variable revenue in Nov/Dec.

Operating Expenses

  • Fixed Costs: $16,167/month baseline.
  • Tiered Costs: Shared Services, Janitorial, and Staffing costs increase with facility use.
  • Variable: $75/session + 5% of revenue.

Capital Plan

  • Initial Capital: $150,000.
  • Contingency: $10k buffer added to each CapEx phase.
  • Phase 1: $64,590
  • Phase 2: $33,500
  • Phase 3: $14,000

Operating Expense Detail

A. Fixed Monthly Costs ($16,167)

Includes NNN Lease ($12k), Property Tax ($1.1k), HOA ($440), Insurance ($350), Tech & Software ($1k), Tech Support ($1k), and Accounting ($250).

B. Tiered Monthly Costs

Costs increase with facility usage. For example, the Shared Services Fee steps down ($16.5k → $11.7k → $9.4k) as corporate oversight lessens, while On-Site Staffing steps up ($2.3k → $3.1k → $4.7k).

C. Variable Costs

Incurred only on use: a blended rate of $75/session for supplies and a 5% transaction fee on collected revenue.

Capital Expenditure Detail

Phase 1: Initial Build-Out ($64,590)

Includes remodeling, painting, furniture, appliances, plants, curtains, a safe, depreciated assets, and a $10k buffer.

Phase 2: Expansion ($33,500)

Includes major construction to combine rooms ($16k), new furnishings, an AED, plants, and a $10k buffer.

Phase 3: Final Furnishings ($14,000)

Covers furniture for the final rooms as they come online, plus a $10k buffer.
